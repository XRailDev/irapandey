About

I’m Russian makeup artist from Tomsk city, Russia. I have been working in beauty industry for over then 8 years.

I have worked with many different beauty contests like: Tomsk Universities beauty contest, “Mrs. Tomsk”, All-Russian contest - “You are unique bride” where I was chief makeup artist, sponsor of the contest, and the member of juries. My work has been appeared in the Russian magazines called: “My Way” (mywaymag.ru), “Dorogoe udovolstvie” (dorogoe.ru).

I have studied and worked under many makeup artists in Russia like Valeria Kutsan (Moscow), Ludmila Tarakanova – LUDOVIK School (Moscow), Maria Lipatnikova – MLStyle School (Novosibirsk) and famous chief makeup artist of Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week, Russia, Andrey Shilkov (Moscow).

Currently, I have my own beauty salon in Tomsk, and looking forward to work in beauty & fashion industry and backstage artist of Fashion Weeks.
